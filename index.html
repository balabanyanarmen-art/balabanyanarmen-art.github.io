enki
“Enki” was my first collaborative shadow theatre project, created with actors Sona Kankanyan and Mher Alebegyan.
In this work, I explored combining the traditional form of shadow theatre with contemporary visual and technical elements, including colored animated backgrounds, recorded narration, and sound design.

Throughout the performance, live theatre, shadow puppets, projection, and sound function together as a single system, creating an animated audiovisual narrative and atmosphere.
